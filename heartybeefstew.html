Classic, Hearty Beef Stew


Description

This hearty beef stew is rich, satisfying, and packed with tender beef, lots of vegetables, and fragrant herbs.

A winter dinner staple, beef stew is the hearty comfort food to make when you need a bowl of stick-to-your-bones, warming goodness. Whether youâ€™re entertaining guests or just looking for a make-ahead meal that will get even better as the week goes on, this recipe is one to keep up your sleeve.


Yields: 8 servings

Prep time: 20 minutes

Total cooking time: 1 hour 30 minutes


Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 yellow onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 3 yellow potatoes, cubed
  • 1 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 cup beef broth, add more if needed
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add meat, onions, and garlic; cook and stir until meat is browned, for 10 to 15 minutes.
  2. Add beef broth, crushed tomatoes, potatoes, and carrots, then stir in paprika, salt and pepper.
  3. Bring to a simmer. Reduce heat to maintain simmer and cook, uncovered, until potatoes are soft, meat is tender, and gravy is thick, about 1 hour.
  4. Serve hot, with cooked white rice or fluffy mashed potatoes.
